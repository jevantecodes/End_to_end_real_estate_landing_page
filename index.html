The problem

The quote process is usually where margin starts leaking.

Investors and GCs lose time, pricing leverage, and visibility when every subcontractor has to reconstruct the same property story from scratch.

01

Repeat site visits waste time

Multiple trades walk the same house because the first round of information was incomplete or inconsistent.

02

Project details stay fragmented

Measurements, photos, notes, and room conditions end up spread across phones, texts, folders, and memory.

03

Limited bid coverage raises cost

When quoting is messy, teams ask fewer subs to bid and lose the price pressure that protects flip margins.

What changes

One site visit becomes a single source of truth.

  • Every trade sees the same floor plans, measurements, and photos.
  • Investors compare bids with cleaner scope alignment.
  • GCs stay in control without chasing missing details all week.
  • Camera visibility keeps the property observable between site visits.

How it works

A tighter workflow from walkthrough to bid comparison.
